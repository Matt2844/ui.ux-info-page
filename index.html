Being able to emphasize with the user is more important than ever.

01

A study in 2000 revealed the average online attention span for someone visiting your website was 12 seconds. Repeated in 2015, it found that the average online attention span has now dropped to 8.25 seconds.

85% of issues related to UX can be detected by performing a usability test on a group of 5 users.
02
Only 1% of users click on slider content. There is a definite decline in the use of this once popular design element, also known as a carousel. According to website user stats, the decline is completely warranted. To encourage user engagement, it might be wiser to turn to other options such as video content or infinite scrolling.
